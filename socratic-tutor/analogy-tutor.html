Finding Analogies
for Hard Problems

This tutor guides you through the process of analogical access — the systematic practice of finding deep structural similarities between your problem and other domains.

  1. Your instructor uploads a set of problems
  2. The tutor presents one and guides you through abstraction
  3. You identify analogous domains from your own knowledge
  4. The tutor suggests additional analogies and you evaluate them together
  5. You receive structured feedback on your reasoning
